About This Project

This platform serves as a comprehensive hub for Human-Centric Artificial Intelligence research projects. Our focus is on creating AI systems that are not only powerful but also interpretable, ethical, and designed with human needs at their core. Each project demonstrates different aspects of human-AI interaction, from explainable machine learning to reinforcement learning with human feedback.

Through interactive interfaces and real-time visualizations, we aim to bridge the gap between complex AI algorithms and human understanding, making artificial intelligence more accessible and trustworthy.
