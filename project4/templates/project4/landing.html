Recommender System Cold-Start Study
Influence of Future Predictions over Active Learning of Users' Tastes

Study Overview

Explore how showing users the impact of their responses affects the learning process in recommendation systems. Our innovative approach provides visual feedback about how your movie ratings will influence future recommendations.

What You'll Do

  • Select movies from our database
  • See how different ratings affect recommendations
  • Provide feedback on the experience
  • Help advance recommendation research

Time Required

  • Study participation: 10-15 minutes
  • Movie selection: 2-3 minutes
  • Feedback: 2-5 minutes
  • Total: ~15-20 minutes
